Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, decision-making, and understanding language.

Type of ai

1.Based on CapabilityNarrow AI (Weak AI): AI designed for a specific task (e.g., Siri, Google Maps). General AI (Strong AI): Can perform any intellectual task like a human (still under research). Super AI: Future concept where AI surpasses human intelligence.
2.Based on FunctionalityReactive Machines: No memory, only react (e.g., Chess-playing computer). Limited Memory: Learns from past data (e.g., self-driving cars). Theory of Mind: Future AI that understands human emotions. Self-aware AI: Advanced concept of AI with consciousness.

Disadvantages

High Cost of Development: Expensive to build and maintain.
Lack of Creativity: AI cannot think beyond programmed data.
Ethical Concerns: Issues like privacy, bias, and misuse.
